Fun Facts About Giraffes

  • Giraffes can run at speeds up to 35 mph.
  • Giraffes only need to drink water once every couple of days.
  • Baby giraffes can stand up and even run within a hour of being born.
  • Giraffes' tongues can be up to 20 inches long and are darkly colored.
  • Despite their characteristic long necks, giraffes actually have the same number of neck vertebrae as humans. Just seven.
